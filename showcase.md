## ⭐ The one that started it all · 起点之作

### Zombie Scavenger · 丧尸清道夫 — by Mx-Shell

An atom-punk robot wanders into a beachfront villa after a zombie
apocalypse, meets a confused ostrich, and breakdances 1980s-style while
kicking a zombie's head across the floor. 3 minutes, ~10 days of work.
Hollywood director PJ Ace called it *"one of the best short films I've
seen in years."*

> 原子朋克机器人在末日丧尸危机后闯进滨海别墅，遇到一只呆鸵鸟，跳着
> 1980 年代霹雳舞、把丧尸头颅踢飞过地板。3 分钟，约 10 天完成。好莱坞
> 导演 PJ Ace 评为"近年来最佳短片之一"。

- Prompt breakdown · 提示词拆解: [prompts/zombie-scavenger.md](./prompts/zombie-scavenger.md)
- The tweet · 推文: [x.com/aibuzhiyu](https://x.com/aibuzhiyu/status/2056426660577288645)
