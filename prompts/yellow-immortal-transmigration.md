## 4 · 运镜规则 / Camera Movement Rules

| 项 | 中文 | English |
|---|---|---|
| **总览** | 多镜头剪辑叙事，共12个分镜，每镜5-8秒，后期剪辑拼接。 | Multi-shot edited narrative, 12 shots total, 5–8 seconds each, assembled in post. |
| **呼吸感** | 手持拍摄，全程保持极其轻微的、如呼吸般的镜头浮动，增强临场感。 | Handheld shot. Throughout, maintain an extremely subtle, breath-like camera float to enhance presence. |
| **角度策略** | 现代段：以车内视角 + 车外跟拍为主，镜头略带晃动，模拟暴雨中行车的不稳感<br><br>穿越后：平视为主，与人物同高，个别镜头用俯视或仰视制造情绪<br><br>黄大仙出场：用低角度仰拍，增强仙气与压迫感 | *Modern segment:* in-car POV + exterior tracking, slight camera shake to simulate the unsteadiness of driving in heavy rain.<br><br>*After transmigration:* eye-level, at the same height as characters. Occasional overhead or low-angle shots for emotional emphasis.<br><br>*Yellow Immortal reveal:* low-angle shot looking up, amplifying immortal presence and quiet authority. |

## 写法选择说明 / Craft Notes

1. **双场景双色调设计**：现代段用冷调青蓝（暴雨夜），古风段用暖调金黄（黄昏村落），形成强烈视觉反差，强化"穿越"感。调色各锁一档，内部统一。
   *Dual-scene dual-palette:* Cool cyan-blue for the modern segment (stormy night), warm golden-amber for the ancient segment (dusk village) — sharp visual contrast reinforces the transmigration feeling. Each scene locks to one grade.

2. **每人两处瑕疵锚点**：易灵风（左眉尾痣 / 右手旧疤）、黄皮子（左耳缺角 / 右前爪浅毛）、石娃（锁骨旧疤 / 脚踝胎记）——确保多镜头中人物一致性，不会中途"换脸"。
   *Two flaw anchors per character:* Yi Lingfeng (eyebrow mole / hand scar), the weasel (ear nick / pale paw), Shi Wa (collarbone scar / ankle birthmark) — locks character consistency across shots.

3. **结尾克制（Rule 6）**：黄大仙现身不搞爆炸、不光芒万丈，而是用"金光中尘埃光柱"和"拂尘无风自动"这种细节来体现仙气，留白给观众想象。
   *Restrained ending (Rule 6):* No explosions, no blinding light for the immortal reveal. Instead, golden light catching dust motes and a drifting horsetail whisk do the work — leaving room for the viewer's imagination.
